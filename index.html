Game Instructions

  • There are a total of 5 locations.
  • Double-click on the map to select a location.

    • yellow if you guessed wrong and will dissapear soon after.
    • red over the correct location, if you guessed wrong and are out of tries.

  • Skipping guesses will be considered the same thing as if you guessed wrong on your last try.
