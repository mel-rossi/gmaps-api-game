Game Instructions

  • There are a total of 5 locations.
  • Double-click on the map to select a location.
  • You have 3 chances to guess each location correctly.
  • Accuracy and speed both count towards your final score.
  • The selection box will turn:
    • green if you guessed correctly.
    • yellow if you guessed wrong and will dissapear soon after.
    • red over the correct location, if you guessed wrong in your last try.
  • You can click the next button before your three guesses are up and skip guesses.
  • Skipping guesses will be counted as the same thing as a wrong guess in your last try.

  • Good Luck!
